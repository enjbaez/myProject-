Enjoli Baez has served in the pharmacy benefit management and prescription insurance industry of Medicare and Medicaid claims for the past 8 years. Her previous work experience was with pharmacy benefit management (PBM) at Medco as a claims analyst in the Long-Term Care department. She remained in that role during the acquisition of Medco by Express Scripts. Later, she joined the Express Script’s Finance team as a Financial Analyst of Medicare prescription claims. Her most recent work experience was at prescription insurance company, Horizon Blue Cross Blue Shield of New Jersey as a pharmacy benefit specialist with the Medicare team. She began that role right after Horizon’s pharmacy team transitioned from PBM Caremark to Prime Therapeutics’. Within that role, her responsibilities included but were not limited to, resolving member inquiries pertaining to benefit cost and access to care, installation of benefit plan codes, collaborating with various internal teams for annual implantation, working with the PBM for a variety of team objectives, initiating processes per CMS guidance, streamlining current processes to increase efficiency, and Prescription Drug Event (PDE) reconciliation reporting.
